Privacy Policy — Send to Google Sheet (Chrome Extension)

Effective date: 2025-09-22

Summary

Send to Google Sheet lets you right-click on a web page and save the selected text plus the page URL/title to a Google Sheet you choose. We don’t run a server, and we don’t sell or share your data.

Data We Process

  • Website content (on user action only): Selected text and the active page’s URL/title.
  • Local settings: Spreadsheet ID, sheet name/column mapping, minimal state (e.g., offline queue). Stored via chrome.storage on your device.

What We Do Not Collect

No PII, health/financial data, web-history lists, user-activity logs, keystrokes, or location. No passwords. OAuth tokens (via chrome.identity) stay on your device and are used only to call Google APIs on your behalf.

How Your Data Is Used

Only to perform the requested action: write selected content and page metadata into your Google Sheet. No analytics or advertising.

Data Sharing

  • Google APIs: Data goes from your browser to your Google Sheet.
  • No third-party sharing or selling.

Security

Least-privilege MV3 APIs (contextMenus, scripting, storage, activeTab, optional notifications/alarms). No remote code, no eval, no externally hosted scripts.

Retention & Control

Data in your Sheet is under your control in your Google account. Local settings can be cleared by removing the extension or clearing extension storage. You can revoke account access anytime.

Children’s Privacy

Not intended for children under 13.
